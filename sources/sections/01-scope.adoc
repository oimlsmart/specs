== Scope

This document specifies the encoding of OIML publications in NISO STS
(ANSI/NISO Z39.102-2022).

It applies to all OIML document types -- International Recommendations (R),
International Basic Publications (B), International Documents (D),
International Guides (G), and Expert Reports (E) -- and to every publication
stage from working draft through published edition.

It does **not** apply to:

* OIML administrative or procedural documents that are not published in the
  OIML document series;
* non-XML representations of OIML publications (e.g. derived PDF or HTML
  renderings);
* vocabularies other than NISO STS 1.2.

Where this document is silent, NISO STS 1.2 applies unchanged.
