== Foreword

The International Organization of Legal Metrology (OIML) is an intergovernmental
treaty organization whose primary aim is to harmonise the regulations and
metrological controls applied by its Member States to measuring instruments
used in trade, health, safety, and environmental protection.

This document, _OIML X 999:2026_, specifies how OIML publications are encoded
in NISO STS (ANSI/NISO Z39.102-2022). It is a derivative work: it draws its
structure and most of its normative content from the _ISO STS Guidelines for
coding ed.2.1_ and adapts that material to OIML's publication context.

This first edition of OIML X 999 was approved by the OIML Technical Assembly
in 2026.
