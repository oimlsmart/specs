== Introduction

OIML publications are currently authored in Metanorma AsciiDoc (using the
`metanorma-oiml` flavor) and rendered to multiple output formats. To enable
OIML publications to participate in the wider NISO STS ecosystem -- the
vocabulary used by ISO, IEC, NISO, and many national standards bodies -- this
document defines OIML's profile of NISO STS 1.2.

This document is intended for:

* authors and editors preparing OIML publications for publication in NISO STS;
* tooling engineers building validation and rendering pipelines against OIML
  NISO STS XML; and
* anyone consuming OIML publications in NISO STS who needs to understand the
  encoding conventions.

The document is normative where it says so. Where it is silent, NISO STS
applies unchanged.
